The Best Movies According to Kavita

My top 3 movies of all-time

Dangal

Dangal is an extraordinary true story based on the life of Mahavir Singh and his two daughters, Geeta and Babita Phogat. The film traces the inspirational journey of a father who trains his daughters to become world class wrestlers.

Bahubali: The Beginning

Baahubali: The Beginning tells the story of Shivudu, a young man who discovers his past and destiny as Mahendra Baahubali, the son of the noble warrior Amarendra Baahubali, and unravels the mystery of his father's death at the hands of the treacherous Bhallaladeva. The film explores themes of royalty, betrayal, and revenge, set against the backdrop of the fictional kingdom of Mahishmati.

RRR

RRR, in the context of the popular Indian film, stands for Rise! Roar! Revolt!. It's an action-packed, fictionalized epic set in 1920s British-controlled India, featuring two legendary revolutionaries who join forces to fight the British. The movie is known for its over-the-top action sequences, musical numbers, and portrayal of themes of friendship and fighting for freedom.
